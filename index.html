Scorched Earth

In this game you must protect your world from aliens who have come to attack your world. You have 100 bullets, 2 minutes and must get at least 25 kills in order to win the game.

How to play:
The goal of the game is to get as many kills before the timmer runs out.
In order to to get kills shoot at the alliens as they go past,
Just make sure you take into account the distance the bullet has to travel, and the fact that you have limited bullets(100).

controls:
Left click or W to shoot
Left arrow or A key to go Left
Right arrow or D key to go Right
